    About Me

    From a very young age I have always been fascinated with computers and technology. I witnessed the beginning of dial up internet, and grew fascinated by how quick and easy it was for people across the world to connect. I learned around that time what HTML was and how it was used to build websites and attempted to build some of my own. During High School I took a class for Visual Basic and HTML which further developed my curiosity.

    However in my early 20s I decided to pursue my other passion - music. After some time I felt I had to make a change and got back into coding again. More recently I've been coding with Python and leaning Web Development with the Coding Bootcamp offered through the University of Arizona.
